About Charter

Since its founding in 1901, Charter has been a club full of tradition. "The Indestructible," as the club is commonly referred to, is today most known for its "Charter Fridays". Charter's social calendar features many noteworthy events including monthly semi-formals, live bands, and weekly parties. Our food, including our bi-weekly pub nights, is consistently ranked some of the best on the street.

Club Week Events, Starting Feburary 2nd

While bickerees are sweating bullets, we'll be chilling with our new sophomores.

Monday: TV/Games Night

Entertainment is important to us. See how Charter does it with late night movies in cozy TV rooms, hectic multiplayer shooters in the computer cluster, and casual board games in the UDR.

Get ready for the largest club week event as sophomores, juniors, and seniors face off in our taproom. Non-alcoholic beverages will also be provided.

Your residential college does study breaks. We do them better with mask-making, pluse a popcorn and candy bar.

We keep it classy here at Charter; this is the first semiformal event of the semester!

Every Friday is Charter Friday. This one will be big.

Club Week culminates in Prohibition Night. This semiformal event, featuring catered food and casino games staffed by professionals, is mandatory for all sophomore members to attend.

Access to the Clubhouse

Open 24/7, our clubhouse features a number of cozy fireplaces, antique leather sofas and spacious wooden tables for all of our members. It's not uncommon to see study groups and group projects to be working late into the night in a clubhouse that makes a rough night a little easier.

Unique People

We're a club with some of the friendliest, most intelligent, and most talented people at Princeton. Looking at the demographics, Charter is made up of 30+ unique majors, has a ratio 45-55 of AB to BSE students, and embraces the owl as the most popular unofficial spirit animal.

Great Food

As an eating club, we know our food. Charter features two pub nights a week, featuring items such as steak, breakfast-for-dinner, and braised pork. Even though each table was made to seat 8 people, it's not uncommon to see a table of 12 trying to fit one more person. Our chefs also make specially prepared meals for members with dietary restrictions.

Wonderful Alumni

Once you are a Charter member, you are a member forever. Every year we see flocks of alumni, clothed in maroon and gold, return to the Indestructible. The friendships in our club span many generations and last a lifetime. Huzzah!
